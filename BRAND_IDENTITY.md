## 1. Brand Logo

### Logo Description
The Riveren logo features a modern, flowing water droplet integrated with elegant typography. The design symbolizes purity, flow, and natural freshness. The droplet contains subtle wave patterns that suggest both river currents and the pristine quality of the water.

## 3. Typography Recommendations

### Primary Font: **Poppins**
- **Usage**: Headlines, brand name, CTAs
- **Weights**: 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
- **Why**: Modern, clean, highly readable, friendly yet professional

### Secondary Font: **Inter**
- **Usage**: Body text, descriptions, forms
- **Weights**: 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- **Why**: Excellent readability, optimized for screens, neutral and versatile

### Font Pairing Example
```css
/* Headers */
font-family: 'Poppins', sans-serif;
font-weight: 600;

/* Body text */
font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;
font-weight: 400;
```
